Panel session at DW Global Media Forum 2026 on malicious AI swarms and democratic elections.

## Topic

The session examined concrete AI-driven threats to electoral integrity and the need for coordinated responses across media, development cooperation, civil society, research, and governance. It focused on malicious AI swarms that can generate persuasive content at scale, coordinate autonomously across online communities, mimic social dynamics, and target civil society through large-scale harassment.
