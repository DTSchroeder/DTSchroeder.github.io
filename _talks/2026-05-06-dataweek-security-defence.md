Panel contribution at Data Week 2026 in Oslo, in the Security track session on trustworthy intelligence, data-driven AI, and Europe's security and defence agenda.

## Session

The session explored how big data, AI, and HPC can support Europe's security and defence priorities in a changing geopolitical environment. The discussion focused on responsible, explainable, trustworthy, and accountable AI for security and defence use cases, including challenges around data sharing, human-centric AI, and the adoption of data-driven systems by public authorities and critical infrastructure actors.

## Format

- Keynotes on trends and needs in data and AI for defence and security, virtual worlds and defence, defence data spaces, and the EDF NEMO project
- Round-table discussion moderated by Christos Gkizelis
- Position pitches by Daniel Sáez Domingo, Daniel Thilo Schroeder, and Antonis Ramfos
