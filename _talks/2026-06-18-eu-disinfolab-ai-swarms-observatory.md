EU DisinfoLab webinar on AI-enabled disinformation risks and the proposal for an AI Influence Observatory.

## Topic

The webinar discussed how large language models and AI agents could add a new layer of risk to the information environment. It focused on emerging threat models including persistent AI personas, AI-supported botnets, cyborg propaganda through verified human accounts, adaptive engagement testing, and campaigns designed to manufacture synthetic public consensus.

The session also covered possible responses: operational taxonomies, curated repositories of cases, simulation environments for studying swarm behavior, behavioral and coordination-based detection signals, prototype AI Shields, and the AI Influence Observatory as infrastructure for making these risks more visible and democratically accountable.
